Pressure is not the problem. Your relationship with pressure is. Most players try to eliminate nerves. That's the wrong goal. Elite performers learn to use that energy — interpret it as excitement, not threat. Three things that actually help: 1. Name the feeling — "I'm excited" not "I'm nervous" 2. Focus on process, not outcome 3. Use a physical anchor — a routine that tells your body: I've done this before What's your pre-match routine? #SportsPsychology #MentalPerformance #FootballMindset
