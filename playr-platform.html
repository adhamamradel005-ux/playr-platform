💡 Something I tell every player I work with: Pressure is not the problem. Your relationship with pressure is the problem. Most young players try to eliminate nerves before a big game. That's the wrong goal. Elite performers learn to use that energy — to interpret it as excitement, not threat. The body doesn't actually know the difference between anxiety and excitement. Same physiological response. What changes is the story you tell about it. Three things that actually help: 1. Name the feeling out loud — "I'm excited" not "I'm nervous" 2. Focus on process, not outcome — what's the first action in this game? 3. Use a physical anchor — a routine that tells your nervous system: I've done this before What's your pre-match routine? Share it below 👇 #SportsPsychology #MentalPerformance #FootballMindset
